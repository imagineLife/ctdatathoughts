Here, Population density is compared to the Average Wage of the top 10 most-populated cities in Connecticut.

Prior to developing this chart, I thought that the higher the population density, the lower the average wage would be. Upon making this chart, and including the option to re-order the bars based on population density, I learned otherwise.

This data is compiled from 2 places:

Population data:

http://portal.ct.gov/DPH/Health-Information-Systems--Reporting/Population/Annual-Town-and-County-Population-for-Connecticut

Wage data:

http://data.ctdata.org/dataset/annual-average-employment-and-wages
